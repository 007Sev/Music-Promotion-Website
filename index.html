Music Promotions For Up And Coming Artists

Music Addicts is a music promotion website designed to help artists share their music and reach new listeners. The goal of this site is to highlight talented artists, promote new releases, and connect fans with great music.

Many independent artists promote their music through platforms like YouTube and SoundCloud. These platforms help artists get discovered and grow their audience around the world.
